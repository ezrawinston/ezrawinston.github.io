how to play

  • find the 5-letter pathword by making a path through the grid, using as few steps as possible!
  • to start, one letter is ruled out, shown in gray. the remaining 25 form a grid
  • first chose your starting position, then choose where to move. you can move up to 3 spaces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • for each letter in your path, you'll find out if it's in the word or not
  • passing over a correct letter reveals all occurrences of the letter in the word will be revealed

tips

  • the answer is never a plural noun ending in "s"
  • the first move can reveal up to 4 spaces
